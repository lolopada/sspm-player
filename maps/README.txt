Place your .sspm files here (Rhythia maps you download from the community).

sspm-player automatically lists all maps in this folder and its subfolders
(unlimited depth). Feel free to organize them however you like:

  maps/
    PackName/
      map1.sspm
      map2.sspm
    Artist/
      Hard/
        map3.sspm

- Press F5 in the menu to refresh the list after adding files.
- You can also drag and drop a .sspm directly onto the window to play it.

Up to 8192 maps can be loaded at once.
